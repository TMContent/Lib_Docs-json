Unfortunately, HP Fortify does not provide an interface to update the custom rulepacks. To update TEAM Mentor custom rulepack you must manually remove it from directory:

%ProgramFiles%\HP_Fortify\HP_Fortify_SCA_and_Apps_4.00\Core\config\customrules

By deleting TEAMMentorRulePack.xml file and Installing the Custom Rulepack again.
